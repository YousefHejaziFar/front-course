Professor Maryam Mirzakhani
About her:

Maryam Mirzakhani (22 May 1356 - 23 July 1396) was an Iranian mathematician and professor at Stanford University. In 2014, Mirzakhani won the Fields Medal, which is the highest award in mathematics, for his work in the field of "dynamics and geometry of Riemannian surfaces and their cubic spaces". She is the first Iranian woman to win the Fields Medal.
